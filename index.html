Hailing from Lawrence Kansas, McCarthy Fitch is a multi-faceted musician now based in South Carolina. Recently having graduated from the College of Charleston School of the Arts, he grounds his style in Jazz Bass. Though, Fitch has taken his experience with guitar, piano, and trombone to expand into a variety of styles and musical crafts such as arranging, composing, as recognized by globally-renowned Jazz artist, Jon Faddis.
